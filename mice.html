MICE

Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Events (MICE) is a type of tourism in which large groups, usually planned well in advance, are brought together for a particular purpose. Ukraine is perfect for this kind of tourism. Infrastructure is growing in Ukraine now what is giving a green light for MICE activities.

Ukraine hosted final of the Football Champion league, Eurovision’s, Euro 2012 and other international contests and competitions. As a result, we have new and updated roads, great terminals in the airports, chains of the world’s major hotel, great flight and train connections with capitals and cities of Europe.

Ukraine can host all year around, it is full of entertainments (such as hunting, shooting, yacht trips, rafting, wine and coffee tours, and others, which can be interesting for different kinds of touristic business groups), great new conference halls for all venues, modern transport.

We know how to do your working trip in Ukraine like it is the best vacation you have ever had!
